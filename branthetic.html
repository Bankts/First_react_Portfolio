Our Engagement Model

Four phases.
No shortcuts.

Every Branthetic engagement follows a structured four-phase model. Phases are not flexible — they exist because structured delivery produces better systems, not faster ones.

Phase 01

Discovery & Alignment

A structured audit of your organisation's product or brand context. We map what exists, what is missing, and what must be built. No design begins before alignment is documented and signed off.

Phase 02

Systems Architecture

Before interfaces are designed, the system is defined. We establish information architecture, user flows, content hierarchy, and component logic. This phase produces the structural blueprint for everything that follows.

Phase 03

Design & Engineering

Interfaces are designed within the system constraints established in Phase 02. Every screen, every component, every state is designed — not approximated. Where applicable, front-end engineering runs in parallel.

Phase 04

Documentation & Handoff

Every engagement closes with full design documentation: component libraries, usage guidelines, technical specifications, and governance notes. We do not hand off files — we hand off operating manuals.

Timeline commitment: Clarity on timeline is established in Phase 01 and is a binding commitment — not an estimate. Product engagements run 8–16 weeks. Brand system engagements run 6–12 weeks.

Common Questions

Answered directly.

"We already have an in-house design team."
We are not a replacement — we are a structural layer. Most of our institutional clients have internal teams that handle execution. What they lack is the systems architecture that makes execution consistent, scalable, and transferable. We build the operating framework that your team works within.
"Your engagement costs are beyond our current budget."
Branthetic's minimum engagement is ₦3M. This reflects the cost of structured, senior-level thinking applied to complex systems — not the cost of design output. If your organisation needs quick, inexpensive design work, we are likely not the right partner. We would rather be clear about this now than begin a conversation that doesn't serve either party.
"Can we start with a smaller scope to test the relationship?"
In specific circumstances, we offer structured advisory sessions as a first engagement. These sessions are paid, scoped, and outcome-defined. If you are unsure whether a full engagement is appropriate, a strategy session is the correct first step — not a reduced-scope project.
"How long do engagements typically take?"
Product design engagements typically run 8–16 weeks. Brand system engagements run 6–12 weeks. Advisory engagements are scoped independently. Timeline clarity is established in Phase 01 and treated as a binding commitment on both sides.
